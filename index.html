Working in the telecommunications sector, i experienced innovative environments and a push for more and more digitalisation in the workplace as well as in products. While developing optimized websites, customer-oriented products and barrier-breaking apps from a business perspective, i really enjoyed working with developers and designers, who always were at the core of success - and with that, a dream grew inside me: i want to design, create and realise ideas myself - ideas that may change and improve the world.


Since 2021, i pursue this dream to make it reality. This gives me the chance to broaden my experience and strengthen my knowledge on designing great digital products.
